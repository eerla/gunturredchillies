Teja S17 Dried Red Chilli

Teja chilli is one of the hottest varieties available, predominantly grown in the southern states of India. Its global popularity is due to the fertile soil and proximity to the Guntur Mirchi Market. Widely known as S17 Teja chilli, it is highly favored by international buyers and is exported in stemless, with stem, and powdered forms. Teja chilli is perfect for use in soups, stir-fries, stews, and spice blends and ideal for long term storage.

Product Details

Color: Deep red with a glossy finish
Skin: Thin yet firm, with fewer seeds
Aroma: Pungent, earthy fragrance
Shape: Pointed tip with med length body
Pungency: High, ideal for spicing up dishes
Processing: Sun-dried, ensuring natural preservation

Specifications

Capsaicin Percentage: 0.589%
Length: 5-7 cm (2-3 inches)
Heat Level: 50,000 to 100,000 SHU
ASTA Color Value: 75-100 (vibrant color rating)
Moisture: 8-10% (ideal for long-term storage)

334 S4/Sannam Dried Red Chilli

The Guntur Sannam chilli is a premium variety known for its vibrant red color and moderate heat, making it perfect for adding both flavor and color to any dish. Grown in the fertile soils of Guntur, Khammam, and Prakasam districts of Andhra Pradesh, this chilli is globally renowned, especially for its consistent quality. Its thick skin ensures a rich, robust flavor when used in powder or crushed form. High in vitamin C and protein, the Guntur Sannam chilli is ideal for health-conscious consumers looking for both taste and nutrition. Widely referred to as S4 Sannam chilli is a top choice for international buyers and perfect for use in spice blends, curries, and sauces. The S4 variety is characterized by its thick red skin, and when crushed, it remains thick, red, and hot.
