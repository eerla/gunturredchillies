Guntur Red Chillies

At Guntur Red Chillies, we specialize in exporting and selling the finest quality dried red chillies and chilli powder. Whether you need chillies for retail or wholesale, we offer the best prices in the market. Our secret lies in sourcing directly from local farmers in the famous Guntur Mirchi Market Yard, known worldwide for producing the spiciest and most flavorful chillies. By working closely with farmers, we ensure that our customers get fresh, high-quality chillies at unbeatable prices. Whether you're looking for bulk orders for your business or smaller quantities for personal use, our chillies will add that perfect kick of heat to your dishes. From mild to extra hot, our varieties suit every taste and need. Exporting worldwide, we take pride in providing fast, reliable service with every order.
