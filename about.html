The Story of Guntur Mirchi Yard

Guntur Mirchi Yard is one of the biggest markets for red chillies in the world. Located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, it started over 60 years ago and quickly became famous for trading some of the spiciest and highest-quality chillies. The climate and soil in Guntur are perfect for growing these chillies, which are known for their bright red color and strong flavor. In the mid-20th century, farmers in the region started growing chillies in large quantities, and the demand grew both in India and abroad. To make it easier for farmers and traders to sell their chillies, the Guntur Mirchi Yard was set up. Over the years, it has become a key place for chilli trading, especially for popular varieties like Teja S17 and 334. Today, millions of tons of chillies are bought and sold at the Yard every year, and they are exported to countries all over the world. The Yard helps support the local economy, providing jobs for many farmers and traders. Thanks to its long history and importance in the chilli trade, Guntur Mirchi Yard remains a major player in the global chilli market.
